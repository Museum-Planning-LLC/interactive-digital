# Humanities field

**Catalog entry** · Digital Exhibits · **humanities template (non-convergence)** · kiosk or full-wall

**Status:** Catalog stub · core not built · 90-day flush target (weeks 10–12)

## Summary

**Constellation / comparison** — visitors bring two or more artifacts, texts, or voices into relation. The system holds **juxtaposition** without collapsing into one lesson or one community convergence.

This is the second **humanities** template beside [Shared Ground](./shared-ground.md). Shared Ground = **bodies in room → convergence**. Humanities field = **visitor selection → comparison field** (pair, triad, constellation).

## Interaction model

- **Compare:** pick A and B (or A, B, C) — related material surfaces in a shared field
- **No forced resolution:** gaps and tension remain visible
- **Not identity-assigned:** visitor choice drives pairing, not demographic tagging
- **Participation types:** touch (kiosk) or multi-user wall with distinct pick zones

Possible material: material culture, literature excerpts, music samples, manuscript facsimiles, oral clips.

## Form factor

| Attribute | Default | Notes |

## Content domains

- Art-adjacent history museums (not fine-art prestige pitch — **material** focus)
- Literary heritage, music history, religious pluralism **without** sermon structure
- University special collections preview rooms
