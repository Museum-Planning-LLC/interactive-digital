# Local history · place

**Catalog entry** · Digital Exhibits · **local history template** · full-wall or kiosk

**Status:** Catalog stub · core not built · 90-day flush target (weeks 7–9)

## Summary

**Place-led** local history — streets, buildings, eras, and fragments surface from **where** the visitor stands or moves on a map or floor plane, not from a single chronological lecture.

Distinct from [Shared Ground](./shared-ground.md): geography and **layer** drive content, not **social geometry between bodies**. Distinct from [flow-field-grid](./flow-field-grid.md): archive and place, not physics simulation.

## Interaction model

- **Place navigation:** map, floor outline, or simplified town grid
- **Layers:** era, community thread, building, event — toggled by position or touch
- **Participation:** visitor chooses **where** to look; system responds with material — multiple threads can coexist without forced merge
- **Authority:** institution supplies archive; visitor assembles proximity — not a single “official story” panel

## Form factor

| Attribute | Default | Notes |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| Tier | **Full-wall** (map at scale) or **kiosk** (neighborhood depth) | Match gallery footprint from study |
| Display | Projection or large touch | Often one strong wall in consolidated gallery |
| Content | Photos, documents, oral clips, captions minimal | Emotional register tags optional (Shared Ground archive pattern) |

## Content domains

- City / county history museums
- Main street, depot, courthouse, waterfront stories
- Post-industrial towns, migration, neighborhood change
