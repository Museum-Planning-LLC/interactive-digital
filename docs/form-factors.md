# Form factors — Digital Exhibits

**Museum Planning LLC** · internal

Three scalable tiers. Menu items and cost bands attach to **form factor + zone + content scope**, not to a single branded product.

## 1 · Kiosk (standalone)

**Description:** Single-station interactive — touch primary; optional simple presence sensing.

**Typical fit:**

- Repositioned gallery with limited footprint or budget
- High visitor throughput, single-user or small-group sessions
- Wayfinding-adjacent or focused topic (one collection thread, one simulation)

**Planning notes:**

- Power, network, and ADA reach at one station
- Enclosure durability for public hours
- Content refresh without full room re-install

**Rough capital band (indicative):** lower tier of digital exhibits; hardware + shell + content package varies widely.

---

## 2 · Full-wall immersive

**Description:** One or more large display surfaces — edge-blended projection or large-format direct view — with immersive content and optional spatial or touch input.

**Typical fit:**

- End wall of a consolidated gallery
- Architectural narrative (timeline, place, process visualization)
- Science visualization at scale (e.g. flow, field, chamber phenomena) without full room wrap

**Planning notes:**

- Sightlines, lighting bleed, and surface treatment
- Speaker placement and ambient audio
- Often pairs with **lower staffing** than multi-table analog exhibits

**Rough capital band:** mid tier; scales with display size, blend, and custom fabrication.

---

## 3 · Full-room immersive

**Description:** Multiple surfaces (often 4–6 walls or wrap), spatial sensing (e.g. depth cameras), ambient sound; visitors’ **movement and relationship** may drive content.

**Typical fit:**

- Signature space in a reactivated wing
- Plural memory or contested place (see [Shared Ground](../catalog/shared-ground.md))
- Communal experience where social geometry matters

**Planning notes:**

- Room volume, projection throw, camera coverage, blackout
- Capacity, egress, and docent/security sightlines
- Highest integration and commissioning effort of the three factors

**Rough capital band:** upper tier; turnkey room installs commonly align with historical single-station interactive exhibit ranges at institutional quality.

---

## Choosing a factor (study → menu)

| Study finding | Likely factor |
|---------------|---------------|
| Consolidate to one gallery bay, tight budget | Kiosk |
| Keep one strong wall, mothball rest of wing | Full-wall |
| Reactivate full room as destination, plural audiences | Full-room |

Form factor is **independent of theme**. Nautical content can ship as kiosk or full-room; Shared Ground’s interaction model assumes full-room but other themes may not.
